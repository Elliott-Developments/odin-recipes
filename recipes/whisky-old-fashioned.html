Whisky Old Fashioned

How to make a Whisky Old Fashioned

This recipe explains how to make a simple Whisky Old Fashioned.

Ingredients

  • Whisky - I find bourbon works the best for me, but any type is good
  • sugar
  • angostura bitters
  • orange
  • maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Add 2 tea spoons of sugar and stir with 2 dashes of angostura bitters and a splash of water
  2. Add the whisky
  3. Cut some orange peel and add to the glass. If you want the bitter taste of the orange around the glass, rub around the rim.
  4. Add a maraschino cherry
  5. Add ice if you want to

Important: When mixing the sugar, make sure it fully dissolves
