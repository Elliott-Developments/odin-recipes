Mingau de Fuba

How to make a traditional Brazilian corn pudding

This recipe explains how to make a very simple dessert which can be made in 10 minutes and with a handful of simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • corn flour
  • milk
  • sugar
  • salt
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of milk to the pan on medium heat, do not allow to boil
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of corn flour to the milk
  3. Add 1 and a half tablespoons of sugar
  4. Add half a tablespoon of salt
  5. Add cinnamon to taste
  6. Remove from pan once desired consistency and serve

Important: Do not stop stirring the ingredients once the corn flour is aded to the pan
