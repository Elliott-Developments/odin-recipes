Super Porridge

How to make a breakfast porridge full of goodness

This recipe explains how to make a porridge in the morning which will set you up for the whole day.

Ingredients

  • thick cut oats
  • milk
  • apple
  • salt
  • cinnamon
  • peanut butter
  • honey
  • dried fruits and/or nuts

Steps

  1. Add 2 cups of milk to the pan on medium heat
  2. Add 2 cups of oats to the milk
  3. Add 1 chopped up apple
  4. Add half a tablespoon of salt
  5. Add cinnamon to taste
  6. Add 1 heaped tablespoon of peanut butter
  7. Remove from pan once desired consistency and serve
  8. Add dried fruits, nuts, or honey to taste

Important: Do not cook on high heat, and stir to avoid sticking to pan
